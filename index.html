Financing

Nowadays, anyone who wants to buy a house, acquire a property or reevaluate existing financing has a variety of financing options.

The existing supply market is large. With the extensive information and general conditions that must be observed, it is particularly difficult to keep an overview and perspective, especially for first-time owners.

Different credit institutions offer different financing concepts that are seldom comparable.

The authorities must separately check whether a project is funded, which can take a few weeks.

The tax laws must also be observed. And only then do you have to decide whether a seemingly good financing offer is also the best for your project.

We will be happy to provide you with non-binding information about possible individual financing of your planned project and put you in touch with our partner banks.

They know DOM Wohn und Gewerbebau GmbH & Co. KG and know exactly how solidly the company builds properties whose shareholders and managing directors are and that DOM Wohn und Gewerbebau - your competent partner
